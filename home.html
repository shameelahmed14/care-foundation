About Us

Care Foundation Thodar is an organization in Thodar that provides engaging active outdoor activities to society. We also offer various medical and social works across Thodar and Helps in financial support to needs. We believe that access to safe society is the most effective and powerful way to ensure sustainable community development worldwide. Thanks to your support, trust, and belief in universal access to safe water, One Drop can increase our scope year after year; the positive impact of our projects grows exponentially.
