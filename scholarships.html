Jindal Foundation Scholarship 2021

Sitaram Jindal Foundation Scholarship 2021 is one of the renowned scholarships among Students. This Scholarship is offered by Sitaram Jindal Foundation. Application in this scholarship can be done after the 11th class to the Postgraduate level. Students from across India for almost all the courses can apply for Sitaram Jindal Foundation Scholarship 2021. The main objective of this scholarship is to provide support to all students who wanted to pursue their studies after 11th but due to financial issues they are not able to study further.


Eligibility For Sitaram Jindal Scholarship 2021

Category A:

Students who have passed 11th or 12th from Govt colleges or govt aided colleges are eligible for this scholarship. Students who Belong to Karnataka & West Bengal should have 75 % Marks ( Boys) & 70 % marks (Girls).

Category B:

Students who are pursuing ITI are also eligible to apply for this scholarship. They should have passed the last exam with a minimum of 50 % Marks. Girls who have passed with 45 % can also participate.

Category C:

Students who have passed 12 & wanted to join the graduation courses Like BA, Bcom, BBA, BCA, BHM, can apply for the Sitaram Jindal Scholarship. Boys with a minimum of 65 % and girls with 60% passing marks can participate in this scholarship. The students who belong to Karnataka & West Bengal should have a minimum of 70 % Marks (Boys) and 65% marks (Girls).

Category D:

Sitaram Jindal Scholarship gives the opportunity to students, doing their post-graduation courses like MA, MCA, MPhil, M.com, MBA, M Sc, and other similar courses can also apply. Students must have obtained 65% (Boys) and 60% (Girls) in their graduation. students who live in Karnataka & West Bengal must have obtained 75 % marks (Boys) & 70 % marks (Girls).

Category E:

Students pursuing their diploma courses in Engineering, Medical & other courses can apply in the Sitaram Jindal Scholarship. They should have obtained 60% marks (Boys ) & 55% marks in their last passed exam.

Category F:

Students interested in pursuing Engineering & Medical courses come under this category. Boys must have obtained 70 % marks & Girls should have obtained 65 % marks in their last passed exam. Boys & Girls living in West Bengal & Karnataka should have 75% marks & 70% marks respectively in the last exam passed.
